Aiming to diversify beyond the wireless handset market Verizon Communications Inc VZ announced Thursday a deal to acquire Skyward a private drone company based in Portland Ore for an undisclosed sum In a press release announcing the deal Verizon said the acquisition of Skyward adds drone operations management to the wireless carrier s Internet of Things portfolio With the handset market getting saturated the four major telecom players have been looking for new growth areas and have been setting their sights on the market for connected devices which is set to explode According to Gartner Research there will be 11 4 billion connected devices by 2018 with spending on IoT security alone forecast to reach 547 million in the same time frame See also Verizon Getting Into IoT With New Wireless Service Verizon said Skyward manages drone operations internationally for companies allowing improved safety and lower operating costs With the deal Verizon said businesses of all sizes will have a single source to integrate manage and wirelessly connect their drone operations Last quarter we announced our strategy to drive innovation and widespread adoption for in flight wireless connectivity through our Airborne LTE Operations ALO initiative a new service to simplify certification and connectivity of wireless drones said Mike Lanman senior vice president for Enterprise Products and IoT at Verizon in a press release announcing the deal This acquisition is a natural progression of our core focus on operating in innovative high growth markets With the addition of Skyward s technology Verizon said it will help companies with the management of drone operations via one platform that can handle all aspects of operating a drone from planning the mission to ensuring the drones are complaint with the Federal Aviation Authority All of this said Verizon is aimed at helping developers and businesses create and manage a wide range of services on Verizon s mobile private network While all of the wireless carriers are eyeing the IoT and drone markets as the next bastions of growth during 2016 Verizon was very active in the area with market research firm
