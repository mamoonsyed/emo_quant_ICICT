Despite better than expected financial performance Yahoo has delayed closing its deal with Verizon the company said on Monday The company said its acquisition by Verizon would close as soon as practicable in the second quarter of this year due to the work required to meet closing conditions In July Verizon agreed to acquire Yahoo s core businesses as it looked to broaden the scale of its AOL media offerings and said it expected the deal to close in the first quarter of 2017
