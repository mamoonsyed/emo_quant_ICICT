With less than three months left for public companies with calendar year ends to implement the FASB s new revenue standard some organizations still seem to be grappling with implementation challenges Some changes have broad accounting implications and will likely require many organizations to apply more judgment to accounting decisions than in the past while other changes to the standard may spill over into operations affecting human resources IT and sales and marketing Eric Knachel and Dennis Howell both with Deloitte Touche LLP discuss issues for organizations to consider as they implement the new standard
