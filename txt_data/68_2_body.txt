Apple Inc said Wednesday it has filed two lawsuits against Qualcomm Inc in China extending a legal battle with its longtime chip supplier into the world s biggest market for smartphones The suits which Apple said it filed on Monday in Beijing s Intellectual Property Court make claims similar to those in a U S lawsuit the iPhone maker filed last week targeting the way Qualcomm sells proprietary technology that is critical for smartphones One of the China suits claims Qualcomm has violated China s antimonopoly law
